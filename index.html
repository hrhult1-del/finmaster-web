Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 19, 2026

FinMaster does not connect to your brokerage or bank account. All trading within the app uses virtual money only. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

FinMaster ("we", "us", or "our") is an AI-powered investing education application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the FinMaster mobile application and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using FinMaster, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Display name (optional, used for leaderboards and certificates)
  • Investing goals and experience level (selected during onboarding)
  • Questions and messages sent to the AI Coach
  • Paper trading activity (virtual trades, portfolio data — no real money)

Information Collected Automatically

  • App usage data (lessons completed, quiz scores, streak data, XP earned)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version, app version)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Push notification interaction data (opens, dismissals)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real financial account information, brokerage credentials, or bank details
  • Social Security numbers or government-issued ID numbers
  • Payment card numbers (all payments processed securely by Apple/Google)
  • Location data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FinMaster Service
  • Personalise your learning experience and AI Coach responses
  • Track your progress, streaks, XP, and achievements
  • Send push notifications you have opted into (including our 7-day onboarding sequence)
  • Process subscription purchases via Apple App Store or Google Play
  • Generate leaderboard rankings and certificates of completion
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve app features and content
  • Respond to support requests

4. Subscriptions and Payments

FinMaster offers optional paid subscriptions (Pro and Elite tiers). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment card information. Subscription management, billing, and refunds are handled through your device's app store account settings.

Subscription prices may vary by region. Your local App Store or Google Play listing shows the price applicable to your region.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your learning progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and, where applicable, on our secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

No security system is impenetrable. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted over the internet.

6. Push Notifications

FinMaster may send push notifications to remind you to continue learning, notify you of price alerts you have set, and deliver our optional onboarding drip campaign. You can opt out of push notifications at any time through your device's notification settings. Disabling notifications will not affect your access to the app.

7. Third-Party Services

FinMaster uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat — Subscription management and purchase validation (revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Alpha Vantage — Market data for paper trading tickers (no personal data shared)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — Payment processing and app distribution
  • Expo / React Native — App framework and crash reporting infrastructure

8. Children's Privacy

FinMaster is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — Opt out of push notifications at any time via device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you delete the app or request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

11. International Users

FinMaster is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using FinMaster, you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by sending a push notification or in-app notice. Your continued use of FinMaster after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. FinMaster does not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us below.

14. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using your personal information is your consent (for optional features like push notifications) and our legitimate interest in providing and improving the Service. You have the right to withdraw consent at any time. For GDPR inquiries, contact our Data Protection contact below.
