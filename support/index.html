💳 Subscriptions & Billing

The free plan gives you access to 3 lessons per day, basic quiz mode, the Paper Trading Simulator with a $10,000 virtual portfolio, and limited AI Coach questions. You can track your streak, earn XP, and view the leaderboard at no cost. Pro and Elite unlock unlimited lessons, real-time AI signals, 2× XP, advanced analytics, and unlimited price alerts.
The free trial offer appears automatically during onboarding. Tap "Start 7-Day Free Trial" and complete the App Store or Google Play payment flow — no charge is made until the trial ends. You can cancel at any time before the 7 days are up and you will not be billed.
Subscriptions are managed by Apple or Google, not by FinMaster directly.
  • iPhone/iPad: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → FinMaster → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → FinMaster → Cancel
After cancellation, you retain Pro/Elite access until the end of the current billing period.
Open FinMaster → Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Restore Purchases". This will re-validate your subscription with Apple or Google and restore your Pro or Elite access. Make sure you are signed into the same App Store / Google Play account that made the original purchase.
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google, not by FinMaster. You can request a refund through:
  • Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google: Google Play Store → Order History → Request Refund
Apple and Google typically approve refunds within 48 hours for recent purchases.
FINMASTER7 is a referral promo code that gives new users 7 days of Pro access for free. Enter it in the app during onboarding or in Settings → Promo Code. The code is single-use per account and cannot be combined with other offers.

📈 Paper Trading Simulator

No. The Paper Trading Simulator uses virtual money only — you start with a $10,000 virtual portfolio. No real money is ever involved. It is purely for educational purposes to help you practice trading strategies without financial risk.
Prices are sourced from Alpha Vantage and may be delayed by up to 15 minutes. They are intended to be realistic for educational purposes, but should not be used for real trading decisions. Past performance in the simulator does not guarantee similar results in real markets.
The simulator supports three order types:
  • Market Order — executes immediately at the current price
  • Limit Order — executes only when the price reaches your target
  • Stop-Loss Order — automatically sells to limit your downside

🤖 AI Coach

No. The AI Coach is an educational tool only. It explains investing concepts, answers questions about lessons, and helps you understand financial topics. It does not provide personalised financial advice, investment recommendations, or tax advice. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making real investment decisions.
AI models can occasionally produce inaccurate information, especially for rapidly changing market data. If you believe an answer is incorrect, please tap the thumbs down icon below the response and describe the issue. Your feedback helps us improve the AI Coach continuously.
Free plan users can ask up to 5 questions per day. Pro and Elite subscribers have unlimited AI Coach access. Your daily question count resets at midnight in your local timezone.

👤 Account & Data

To delete your account and all associated data: open FinMaster → Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". This will permanently delete your progress, XP, streak, and all personal data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, cancel it first through your App Store or Google Play settings.
Streaks are calculated based on daily lesson completions in your local timezone. If you believe your streak was incorrectly reset due to a technical issue (e.g., app crash, timezone change), contact us at support@finmaster.app within 48 hours with your username and the date of the issue. We review streak restoration requests case-by-case.
Your progress is stored locally on your device. If you delete and reinstall the app, your progress will be reset. We recommend completing lessons consistently rather than relying on backup. Cross-device sync is on our roadmap for a future update.
